# Analytics & Results Summary
**Project:** Transformers: Forged by Primus  
**Published:** July 13, 2026  
**Promotion:** None (organic traffic only)  
**Source:** Platform analytics (30-day view; primary activity concentrated in the first week after publish)

---

## Overview

*Forged by Primus* was published publicly on a multi-script AI roleplay platform with no advertising or external marketing. The goal of early deployment was practical validation: whether a complex, structured RPG system could attract organic engagement and operate under live conditions.

---

## Live Metrics

| Metric | Value | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Page Views | 74 | Cumulative views in the observed window |
| Daily Visitors | 37 | Average daily unique visitors |
| Chats Opened | 19 | Distinct chat sessions |
| Messages Sent | 98 | Total messages across all chats |
| Daily Chatters | 13 | Average daily active chatters |
| Avg. Time on Page | 6 minutes | Average page session length |
| Avg. Chat Length | 1 minute | Average chat duration |
| Favorites | 8 | Platform favorite / heart count at capture |

Traffic pattern shows a clear launch spike around publish, followed by lower sustained activity — typical for an unpromoted niche listing.

---

## Interpretation

These figures come from a short, unpromoted window and should be read as early signals:

- The system attracted organic traffic quickly after publish
- A meaningful share of visitors progressed from page view into chat activity
- Daily chatters and favorites indicate more than pure bounce curiosity
- Average time on page (6 minutes) is stronger than average chat length (1 minute), suggesting some users evaluated the listing carefully even when they did not sustain long sessions
- Most importantly, the deployment created real external conditions for observing structured prompt systems under live use

The data supports deployability and early interest. It does not yet support strong claims about retention, conversion quality, or long-term engagement.

---

## What This Demonstrates

- A multi-script, structured RPG system can be published and used publicly
- Core systems were exercised under actual usage conditions rather than only internal testing
- Organic engagement occurred despite relative complexity and zero marketing
- The project moved from internal development into live external validation

---

## Limitations

- Short observation window after publish
- Limited sample size
- Audience restricted to organic discovery
- Platform metrics do not include detailed qualitative feedback or retention cohorts
- Average chat length remains low; listing interest does not automatically equal deep session quality

---

## Summary

Published July 13, 2026 with no promotion, *Forged by Primus* recorded 74 page views, 19 chat sessions, 98 messages, and 8 favorites in the early live window. These results show deployability, basic usability, and organic interest under real conditions. They are best read as validation of readiness for broader testing and iteration, not as mature product metrics.
