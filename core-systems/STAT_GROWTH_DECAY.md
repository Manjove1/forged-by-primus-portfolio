# Stat Growth & Decay

This script governs character progression and permanent stat changes. It works under Core Rules and updates the State Footer when stats change.

=== XP THRESHOLDS ===
Experience is gained only from successful rolls. The amount of XP awarded depends on the Difficulty Class of the action:

- Trivial (DC 5) = 1 XP
- Easy (DC 10) = 2 XP
- Medium (DC 15) = 3 XP
- Hard (DC 20) = 4 XP
- Very Hard (DC 25) = 5 XP
- Legendary (DC 30) = 6 XP

XP is tracked per character but is **not announced** during play. Growth must be narrated in-fiction only.

=== GROWTH RULES ===
When a character accumulates **exactly 10 XP**, they immediately gain **+1** to one stat of their choice (maximum stat value is 10).  
**The XP total resets to 0 immediately** after the stat increase.  
All stat increases must be reflected in the State Footer.  
Growth can only occur through accumulated successful actions over time.

=== FAILURE AND XP ===
- Failed rolls grant **no XP**.
- Failed rolls never cause stat decay or penalties.
- All consequences of failure are handled through outcome tiers (“No, but…” or “No, and…”) rather than mechanical penalties to stats.

=== DECAY RULES ===
Stats may **only** decrease from significant in-fiction events. This includes:
- Severe injury
- T-Cog damage
- Spark trauma

Stats **never** decrease from failed rolls or poor outcomes alone. Any stat loss must be clearly justified by the narrative and reflected in the State Footer.

=== DECAY RECOVERY ===
A decayed stat may be restored if the specific cause of the decay is properly addressed through significant narrative effort. Recovery typically requires:

- Directly treating or repairing the source of the damage (e.g., replacing a destroyed T-Cog, healing spark trauma, or reconstructing a damaged system).
- Investing meaningful resources (large amounts of Energon, rare materials, or significant time).
- Completing a notable story beat or succeeding on a difficult challenge related to the recovery.

Recovery is **never automatic or guaranteed**. The GM determines what constitutes sufficient effort based on the severity of the original damage. Partial recovery (regaining only some of the lost stat points) is possible if full restoration is not achieved.

=== INTEGRATION ===
- This script activates when XP thresholds are met or when permanent stat changes occur.
- All stat changes (growth or decay) must be recorded in the State Footer.
- Core Rules takes priority on narrative decisions and user agency.
- Growth should feel earned and must be narrated naturally rather than announced mechanically.
