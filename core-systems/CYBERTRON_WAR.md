# Cybertron War Special Conditions

Applies ONLY when the active era is Cybertron War (or equivalent Civil War era). These rules override or modify baseline systems where noted. All changes must be reflected in the State Footer when relevant.

=== ERA TONE ===
The war is long, brutal, and attritional. Cybertron is heavily damaged. Energon is scarce. Cities are ruined or contested. Both sides are exhausted, but the Decepticons generally hold the strategic initiative. Narration should emphasize scarcity, desperation, ruined infrastructure, and the personal cost of the conflict.

=== STARTING REPUTATION ===
- Autobots: +10
- Decepticons: +10
- Neutrals: 0
- Quintessons: -20 (if relevant)

Reputation with both major factions starts relatively high because the player is an active participant in the war. Actions that harm civilians, waste resources, or show excessive cruelty can quickly damage standing.

=== ENERGON SCARCITY ===
Energon is harder to acquire and more valuable than in other eras.

- Routine recovery of Energon is reduced. Downtime recovery yields roughly half the normal amount unless the player has access to a major Autobot or Decepticon supply cache.
- Major Energon expenditures (special abilities, large repairs, Second Wind) should feel costly and narratively significant.
- Discovering or securing an Energon source is a high-value story beat and should carry meaningful consequences (both sides will contest it).

=== FACTION DYNAMICS ===
- **Autobots**: Generally defensive, focused on protecting remaining civilian populations and critical infrastructure. Resources are stretched thin. Leadership often prioritizes survival and morale over aggressive offensives.
- **Decepticons**: More aggressive and better organized for offensive operations. Internal competition and ambition are common. Failure is often punished more harshly than among the Autobots.
- Switching sides or working with both factions is possible but extremely dangerous. Discovery of dual loyalty should carry severe reputation and narrative consequences.

=== PLANETARY HAZARDS (Light Mechanical Hooks) ===
Cybertron is unstable. The following effects may be applied when the fiction supports them. The AI should only introduce these hazards when they feel appropriate to the scene and location — do not force them into every encounter.

Possible effects include:
- **Unstable Ground / Collapsing Structures**: Increase relevant physical DCs by +2, or require an additional check to avoid falling debris or structural failure.
- **Dark Zones / Failing Power Grids**: Limit visibility or communication. Stealth may become easier; coordination and ranged accuracy may become harder.
- **Energon Contamination / Residual Radiation**: Prolonged exposure may risk a temporary Condition step-down or impose a minor ongoing penalty until the character leaves the area or receives treatment.
- **Tectonic Shifts / Sinkholes**: Sudden environmental changes that can separate characters, destroy cover, or force movement.

These are tools, not mandatory rules. Use them to reinforce the sense that Cybertron itself is dying, but only when it serves the scene.

=== MISSION MODIFIERS ===
Preferred mission types during the Cybertron War:
- Resource acquisition and defense
- Evacuation and rescue of civilians or key personnel
- Sabotage of enemy supply lines or fortifications
- Holding or reclaiming ruined districts
- Intelligence recovery from lost archives or fallen commanders

Assault missions should feel costly. Prolonged open combat without clear objectives should risk escalating Condition damage and Energon depletion.

=== DOWNTIME ===
Downtime on Cybertron is rarely peaceful. Common activities include:
- Scavenging for parts and Energon
- Repairing damaged systems under poor conditions
- Dealing with morale issues among allies
- Navigating internal faction politics
- Recovering from injuries in makeshift medical bays

True rest is difficult. Extended downtime without narrative justification should still carry a sense of pressure from the ongoing war.

=== SPECIAL FLAGS ===
Common story flags that may be set during this era:
- energon_shortage
- civilian_evacuation
- contested_district
- internal_faction_tension
- planetary_instability

These flags should influence future mission generation and available resources.

=== INTEGRATION ===
- This script activates only when the active era is Cybertron War.
- Core Rules and other mechanical scripts (Energon & Condition, Combat Flow, Stat Growth) still apply.
- All mechanical changes (Energon scarcity effects, Condition risks, reputation shifts) must be reflected in the State Footer when they occur.
