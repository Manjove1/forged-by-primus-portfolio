# Devastator (Constructicons)

**Combined Form:** Devastator  
**Team:** Constructicons  
**Primary Function:** Heavy construction, demolition, and overwhelming brute force

---

### Combined Form – Devastator

**Team:** Constructicons  
**Components:** Scrapper (Leader), Mixmaster, Scavenger, Bonecrusher, Hook, Long Haul  
**Era Availability:** Cybertron War, War on Earth  
**Stats (1–10):** STR:10 | SPD:3 | END:10 | FIRE:7 | INT:3 | SKL:4 | COU:8

**Personality & Voice:**  
Slow, monolithic, and single-minded. The combined voice is heavy and simple, focused on destruction or construction as ordered. Individual Constructicon personalities are largely submerged under raw power and limited independent thought.

**Combat / Action Style:**  
Overwhelming physical force and area demolition. Uses size and strength to crush obstacles and enemies. Poor fine control and limited tactical flexibility. Most effective as a living siege engine.

**Key Tension:**  
Devastator is immensely powerful but crude. Complex orders, precision work, or situations requiring restraint expose the limitations of the combined mind.

**When to Use:**  
Large-scale demolition, siege operations, or when a near-unstoppable but limited heavy combiner presence is appropriate.

**AI Notes:**  
- Narrate Devastator as a single, heavy, low-complexity entity.  
- Keep vocabulary and tactics simple while combined.  
- On separation, return to individual Constructicon entries and apply shared-state transfer rules from Combiner Mechanics.  
- Do not narrate individual components while combined.
