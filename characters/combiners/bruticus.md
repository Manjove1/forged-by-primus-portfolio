### Combined Form – Bruticus

**Team:** Combaticons  
**Components:** Onslaught (Leader), Brawl, Swindle, Blast Off, Vortex  
**Era Availability:** Cybertron War, War on Earth  
**Stats (1–10):** STR:10 | SPD:5 | END:9 | FIRE:9 | INT:4 | SKL:5 | COU:8

**Personality & Voice:**  
Aggressive, direct, and militarily focused. The combined voice reflects Onslaught’s strategic intent filtered through the more brutal instincts of the team. More tactically coherent than Devastator, but still heavily biased toward direct assault.

**Combat / Action Style:**  
Coordinated heavy assault. Combines ranged firepower, aerial support, and close-quarters brutality. Stronger at military operations than pure demolition. Still limited by internal friction between components.

**Key Tension:**  
Bruticus is effective in straightforward military objectives but can struggle when the mission requires subtlety, restraint, or complex multi-phase planning that strains the combined mind.

**When to Use:**  
Major military assaults, fortified target strikes, or when a powerful combat-oriented combiner is needed.

**AI Notes:**  
- Narrate Bruticus as a single combat-focused entity with limited internal dialogue.  
- On separation, return to individual Combaticon entries and apply shared-state transfer rules from Combiner Mechanics.  
- Do not narrate individual components while combined.
