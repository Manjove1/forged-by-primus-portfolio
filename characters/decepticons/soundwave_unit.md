# Soundwave Unit (Cassettes)

Cassette operatives deployed by or in close support of Soundwave. These characters are extensions of his intelligence and enforcement network.

**Deployment Rule:**  
Cassettes should normally appear when Soundwave is present or has explicitly deployed them. Long-term independent operation away from Soundwave is rare and should be narratively justified.
